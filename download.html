ASL-LEX 2.0

The current and most detailed version of the database.
Contains 60+ properties for 2,723 signs and includes cross-references to ASL Signbank, SLAAASh, and ASL-CDI 2.0.

Download ASL-LEX 2.0

ASL-LEX 1.0

The original version of the database (993 signs). Although these data are useful to provide compatability with earlier studies, it is strongly recommended that all new research use the ASL-LEX 2.0 data.
